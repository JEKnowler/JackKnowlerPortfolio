About the Game

Scrambled Eggs is an engaging educational game designed to help learners practice sequencing and ordering skills in a playful way. Players are tasked with arranging eggs in the correct order, encouraging logical thinking and problem-solving while keeping gameplay fun and interactive. This game blends learning objectives with lighthearted mechanics to create an enjoyable experience.
